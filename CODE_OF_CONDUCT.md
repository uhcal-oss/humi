# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to providing a welcoming and inclusive environment for all contributors, users, and community members of the HUMI v2 Plant Management System project, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and expression, level of experience, education, socio-economic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

We are dedicated to fostering an open and respectful community where everyone feels valued and heard.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to creating a positive environment include:

- **Respectful Communication** — Using welcoming and inclusive language free from discriminatory remarks
- **Active Listening** — Considering different perspectives and learning from others' experiences
- **Constructive Feedback** — Providing and receiving criticism gracefully and focusing on improvement
- **Inclusivity** — Welcoming and supporting people of all backgrounds and experiences
- **Professionalism** — Maintaining a professional tone in all interactions, especially during disagreements
- **Attribution** — Giving proper credit to contributors and respecting intellectual property
- **Collaboration** — Working together toward shared goals and community success

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:

- Harassment, intimidation, or discrimination of any kind
- Offensive comments related to personal characteristics
- Unwelcome sexual attention or advances
- Trolling, insulting/derogatory comments, and personal or political attacks
- Public or private harassment or threats
- Publishing others' private information without explicit permission (doxxing)
- Spamming or abuse of project resources
- Any conduct that would be considered harmful or hostile

## Enforcement Responsibilities

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing standards of acceptable behavior. They have the right and responsibility to remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, code, wiki edits, issues, and other contributions that violate this Code of Conduct.

## Reporting Guidelines

Instances of unacceptable behavior should be reported to the project maintainers:

- **Primary Contact**: [Batuhan Türkoğlu] (batuhantrkgl)
- **Email**: Please use GitHub issues marked as confidential or contact via direct message

All reports will be:
- Treated confidentially
- Reviewed promptly and fairly
- Investigated thoroughly
- Handled with sensitivity to the reporter's privacy and safety

Retaliation against anyone for reporting concerns in good faith is strictly prohibited.

## Enforcement Actions

Project maintainers may take the following actions in response to code of conduct violations:

1. **Warning** — A private message explaining the conduct violation and its impact
2. **Temporary Restriction** — Temporary removal from project interaction (24-48 hours)
3. **Permanent Ban** — Removal from the project community and all future participation

The severity of the violation and pattern of behavior will determine the response.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to all interactions related to the HUMI v2 project, including:
- Project repositories and discussions
- Issue trackers and pull requests
- Project documentation and wikis
- Public project discussions and communications
- Community events and meetings
- Private communications about the project

We may update this Code of Conduct as our community evolves. Any changes will be announced publicly and all community members will be notified.
